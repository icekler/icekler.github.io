## Prints

For printing I'm currently using two services: [Printful](https://www.printful.com) and [Graphic.jp](https://www.graphic.jp/). They both have pros and cons, so I'll list them in [Printful.com details](#printfulcom-details) and in [Graphic.jp details](#graphicjp-details).

<a id="pricing-scheme"></a>
### Pricing scheme

In a nutshell, my pricing is calculated like this:

```
Order cost + Shipping cost + Labor price
```

The order cost depends on what quality, size, and extra content is included in the order. I do not control this, but rather it's the cost of using [Printful.com](https://www.printful.com) and [Graphic.jp](https://www.graphic.jp/) to do the print.  
The shipping cost is either calculated by Printful or, in the case you use Graphic.jp or want a signed piece, it depends on where you live, since I'll be doing the shipping myself.  
The labor price is as follows:

- Ink paintings, black and white paintings, "Ink and Digital Filters" paintings: 10 USD (1,099 JPY)
- Watercolor paintings, acrylic paintings, color paintings: 20 USD (2,198 JPY)
- Large scale acrylics, oils : 40 USD (4,396 JPY)

***Payment is requested via PayPal after the order is consolidated***

<a id="printfulcom-details"></a>
### Printful.com details

*pros*:

- Can choose to print Canvas, framed posters, plain posters, or even cups, shirts, and other assorted items
- Can ship worldwide, and probably arrives faster [(with exceptions to some countries due to legal restrictions)](https://help.printful.com/hc/en-us/articles/360014066779-Is-Printful-available-in-all-countries-)
- Eventually I'll be able to automate it

*cons*:

- I can't sign the piece or attach personalized thank you letter (unless I ship it to myself and ship again)
- I can't print high quality glass prints or special materials
- I can't do a varnish finish on canvas prints

*notes*:

- All the sizes are in Imperial units (inches).
- Shipping cost is calculated by Printful.com
- I can send a personalized letter separately from the piece, if requested.
- I have to prepare each file for printing (specially for canvas prints)

<a id="graphicjp-details"></a>
### Graphic.jp details

*pros*:

- I can print high quality gliceé, glass printing, canvas printing, poster printing.
- I can do a varnish finish on canvas prints
- I can send personalized thank you letters, signed pieces, etc.
- I can ship worldwide, but I have to ship it myself. (see cons)

*cons*:

- I have to ship it myself (unless you live in Japan), so it takes longer to arrive and the shipping is more expensive
- I can't print on cups, shirts, or any assorted items, the shop is mostly for flat prints.
- I can't ever automate it

*notes*:

- All the sizes are in Metric units (millimeters)
- I have to calculate the cost myself, and we arrive to a price after a discussion of what the order includes.
- I have to prepare each file for printing (specially for canvas prints)
- Shipping costs are calculated by Japan Post [(international shipping)](https://www.post.japanpost.jp/int/charge/list/index_en.html) [(national shipping)](https://www.post.japanpost.jp/index_en.html)
